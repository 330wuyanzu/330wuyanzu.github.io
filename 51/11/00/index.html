    SRM 中对"损伤"damage一词有特别说明：

    损伤包括结构性部件横截面上一切和所有永久性的变形或变化

    # 分类

    损伤通常由四大类原因导致：

    • 机械作用Mechanical action
    • 化学或电化学反应Chemical or electro-chemical reaction
    • 热力作用或温度循环Thermal action or cycling
    • 冶金特性(材料工艺)Inherent metallurgical characteristics

    SRM 中，将损伤分为可修理损伤Repairable和不可修理损伤Non-repairable:

    • 可修理损伤分为允许的损伤和不允许的损伤(需要立即修理)
    • 不可修理损伤指的是无法对损伤进行修理，只能通过更换整体结构部件来达到修理的目的

    # 不可修理损伤的类型

    # Scratch - 划伤

    机械作用损伤，通常由尖利的物体引起：

    A scratch is a line of damage of any depth and length in the material which causes a cross-sectional area change

    # Gouge - 凿伤

    机械作用损伤，通常由相对尖利的物体引起：

    A gouge is a damage area of any size which results in a cross-sectional area change. It is usually caused by contact with a relatively sharp object which produces a continuous, sharp or smooth channel-like groove in the material.

    # Mark

    机械作用损伤：

    A mark is a damaged area of all sizes where a concentration of scratches, nicks, chips, burrs or gouges etc. is shown. You must prepare the damage as an area and not as a series of individual scratches, gouges etc.

    # Crack - 裂纹

    机械作用、热力作用或温度循环损伤：

    A crack is a partial fracture or complete break in the material.

    # Dent - 凹坑

    机械作用损伤：

    A dent is a damaged area which is pushed in, with respect to its usual contour. There is no cross-sectional area change in the material, area edges are smooth.

    # Nick - 缺口、裂口

    机械作用损伤：

    A small decrease of material due to a knock etc. at the edge of a member or skin.

    # Distortion - 扭曲、变形

    机械作用、热力作用或温度循环损伤：

    Any twisting, bending or permanent strain which results in misalignment or change of shape. May be caused by impact from a foreign object, but usually results from vibration or movement of adjacent attached components. This group includes bending, buckling, deformation, imbalance, misalignment, pinching, and twisting.

    # Corrosion - 腐蚀

    化学或电化学反应、冶金特性材料工艺导致：

    The destruction of metal by chemical or electro-chemical effect. Rust is oxidation or corrosion of metallic alloy part containing ferrous materials.

    # Crease - 折皱

    机械作用导致：

    A damaged area which is pushed in or folded back on itself. The edges are sharp or well specified lines or ridges.

    # Abrasion - 磨损

    机械作用、热力作用或温度循环导致：

    An abrasion is a damage area of all sizes which causes change in a cross-sectional area because of scuffing, rubbing, scraping or other surface erosion. It is usually rough and irregular.

    # Debonding - 脱胶、剥落

    机械作用、化学或电化学反应、热力作用或温度循环导致：

    Debonding is when a separation of materials occurs due to an adhesive failure

    # Delamination - 分层

    机械作用、化学或电化学反应、热力作用或温度循环导致：

    Delamination is when the separation of plies occurs in a multi-laminate material. This can be caused by the material being hit - Impact Delamination, or when there is a resin failure for any other reason

    # Fretting

    机械作用导致：

    Surface damage at the interface between elements of the joints resulting from very small angular or linear movements. Evidence of fretting is usually the production of fine black powder staining

    # Indentation - 压痕

    机械作用导致：

    Indentations are pressure marks which typically show regular smooth deformation of the surface without change of the cross sectional area

    # Burn Mark - 烧痕

    热力作用、温度循环导致，一般都是雷击：

    A localized indication of excessive heating ( paint and/or surface black discoloration) after lightning strike. Scorch mark, pitting or local melt thru in addition to paint and/or surface black discoloration can be seen on metallic skins or fasteners. On composite structures, in addition to paint discoloration, puncturing or delamination/disbonding can occur. The size limit for visual inspection is the limit of paint discoloration

    金属材料烧蚀

    螺钉烧蚀

    复合材料烧蚀

    # Penetration - 穿透

    机械作用导致：

    部件表面被穿破，或部件被扎进内部

    A penetration is an area of a component's surface that has been pierced through or into its interior
